workflow:
1. Acquire .mp3
1B resample, export constant bit rate @128k,144k,192k bps (from 250k from Amazon) - from xLNc 4.x video
2. Listen several times, formulate general vision/style/mood of song
3. Trim out unwanted parts with Audacity
4. Level out volume (select all, Effect -> Compressor)
5. Add timing cues (labels) in Audacity during playback
